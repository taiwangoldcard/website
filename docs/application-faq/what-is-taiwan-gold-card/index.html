What is Taiwan Employment Gold Card?

Taiwan Employment Gold Card is a special visa launched in 2018 to attract “special” talents in Taiwan. It gives you the right to stay and work in Taiwan from 1 year up to 3 years (renewable). You’ll be able to work for any company, start a new business, have great tax incentives, benefit Taiwan healtchare system, and bring your family with you.

The application is online, and straigh forward. Approbation is based on your existing skills. You don’t need to already have a job or any company to sponsor you.
